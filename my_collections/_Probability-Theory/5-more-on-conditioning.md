## Continuous Stuff

$$
f_{X \vert Y}(x \vert y) = {f_{X,Y}(x,y) \over f_Y(y)}, \\
P(X\in A \vert Y=y) = \int_A f_{X\vert Y}(x \vert y)dx. $$
